Welcome to the BeSCI Research Group at [Kingston Business School](https://www.kingston.ac.uk/faculties/faculty-of-business-and-social-sciences/schools/kingston-business-school/).

## About Us

Our research focuses on the role of social, physical and mental processes in decision-making, uncertainty judgements and creative thinking.

We use a combination of experimental and observational methods to study both theoretical and applied questions in behavioural science.
