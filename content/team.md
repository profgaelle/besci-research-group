## Join Us

We welcome volunteers to support our behavioural science research. Volunteers gain training and experience in experimental methods, data preparation, coding and analysis.

**What works well:** We find that volunteers who can contribute approximately one day per week over a teaching block (minimum one term) gain the most from the experience and can meaningfully contribute to ongoing projects. However, we're happy to discuss flexible arrangements.

To apply, please email a CV and statement about your interest and relevant skills to: [g.vallee-tourangeau@kingston.ac.uk](mailto:g.vallee-tourangeau@kingston.ac.uk?subject=[BeSCI]%20Volunteer%20Application)
