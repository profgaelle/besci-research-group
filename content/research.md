## Research Themes

### Decision-Making & Judgement

How do social, physical and mental processes shape our decisions under uncertainty?

### Creative Cognition

Understanding the cognitive and environmental factors that support creative thinking and problem-solving.

### Applied Behavioural Science

Studying real-world applications including healthcare decisions, sustainable consumption, and organisational behaviour.

## Methods

We use:

- Online experiments and surveys
- Interactive lab experiments
- Eye-tracking studies
- Video-based behaviour observations
- Process-tracing via remote screen-sharing
